SpeakEasy

A New Way to Browse with Your Voice

With SpeakEasy, you can navigate the web with simple vocal commands. These commands are displayed in the scrolling menu located on the right side of every webpage.

Page Navigation

The following is a list of possible commands:

    "Scroll down" - Navigate further down the webpage.
    "Scroll up" - Navigate further up the webpage.
    "Go back" - Return to the previous webpage.
    "Go forward" - (If you have navigated to a previous page) Return to the following page.
    "Refresh" - Reload the current page.
    "New URL" - Navigate to a new website. To perform this command, say "New URL" followed by the name of the website and its domain.

      Ex. "New URL google.com"
    "New tab" - Opens a new tab that directs to Google.

For all other commands, just say them as they are displayed in the menu. Longer links will include an identifier in their title such as "Link X". For these links, just say "Link" and the associated number.
